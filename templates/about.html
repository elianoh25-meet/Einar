About Einar Youth Center

Empowering Palestinian Arab youth in Jerusalem with academic and career support.

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing space for Palestinian Arab youth in Jerusalem to develop their academic and professional skills. Through our innovative programs and supportive environment, we help young people reach their full potential and become leaders in their communities.

Our mission is to:

  • Bridge the educational gap for Palestinian Arab youth in Jerusalem.
  • Equip young people with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in college and careers.
  • Foster a sense of belonging and community among Palestinian Arab youth in Jerusalem.
  • Empower young people to become positive changemakers in their communities.

If you are interested in learning more about our programs or how you can get involved, please contact us.
